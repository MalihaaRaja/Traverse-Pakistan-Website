What we are

Traverse Pakistan is a trusted name in the arena of cultural tours, adventure, and nature tourism in Pakistan. At the "Crossroads of Asia", Pakistan is home to a multitude of historic, geographic and cultural wonders. The Subcontinent’s history dates back to the stone-age era of the late 4th millennium BC. The centuries since have seen countless civilizations, tribes, explorers and empires who have fought their battles, built their religious monuments and otherwise imprinted the landscape with their rich and vibrant stories. Pakistan hosts an impressive array of cultural treasures – ancient Buddhist ruins, lost civilization sites, priceless Gandhara art, Alexander the Great legacies, and Mughal Empire remains abound throughout the country. The mountainous Northern Areas of Pakistan, in particular, provide breathtaking opportunities for adventure, nature, and cultural tourism, where in tourists experience the historic and ancient Silk Route carved alongside the mighty Indus River between China and Pakistan.
